World Mental Health Month October 10, 2023

The Office of Guidance Counseling and Services leads the World Mental Health Celebration every 10th day of October. The theme for this year's celebration is "Mental Health is a Universal Right", as presupposed by the World Foundation of Mental Health. The mental health campaign and awareness program are scheduled from October 9 until 13, 2023, on all Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College (ISPSC) campuses. This day, October 10, 2023, the celebration of the program takes place at the ISPSC Santa Maria Campus College Gymnasium. This program features the ever active resource speaker, Mrs. Princess D. Racca, RGC - Head of Guidance Counseling and Services. With the participation of the College of Computing Studies (CCS) student leaders, classroom officers, and the officers of the Computing Society of the Philippines (CSP) of Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College, Santa Maria Campus, they actively participate in spreading awareness about mental health and making a positive change towards mental health.

Teacher's Day Celebration OCTOBER 5-6, 2023

From cultural shows to team-building exercises and sporting activities, WE HAVE IT ALL. The teachers' day celebration of the College of Computing Studies (CCS) in Sta. Maria Campus, with the theme "Celebrating the Day of the Backbones of All Professions," is a challenging yet successful day of showing the love, gratitude, and support of the students, staff, and teachers for the event. With the inspirational thoughts from the ISPSC Sta. Maria College Administrator: Dr. Francisco N. Divina and the participation of the teachers of the CCS, the program was successfully delivered, and the teachers were pleased to enjoy the event. Once again, happy Teacher's Day to all the faculty members and staff of the CCS department. This event may not have been possible without the hard work and efforts of the newly elected, hardworking, and very active Computing Society of the Philippines (CSP) officers, headed by the CSP President, Hillary Buis, together with their adviser, Mrs. Cherry Lynn Navato.

ISPSC Santa Maria Campus - Two technocrats delivered their evocative words, which resonated with the judges and audience alike for the Spoken Poetry competition during the Teachers' Day Celebration on October 6 at the College Gymnasium. Ms. Arden Liwan, BSIT 4B, emerged as the champion whose performance was a masterful display of emotion and expression.
