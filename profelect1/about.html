Me, Myself and I

Student/Mother/Wife/"Trying Hard Web Developer"

I am Rowena Garais, 32 years old, a mother of two and currently studying at Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State College, Santa Maria Campus. I am taking up Bachelor of Information Technology major in Web Development. As an IT student, I can say I have almost experienced the hardship of being an "IT STUDENT". They say "ang IT ay pindot-pindot lang" but based on my own experienced "IT" is not just "pindot-pindot lang pala". IT course is just like an "ENGINEERING" that involves planning of designs you want to your web page that you must have enough knowledge in coding and how to organize them. Sleepless nights will be spent and you will be suffering of giving up..That you might think that you cannot survive...Little mistakes that made your code error and you don't have any idea how to debug it then you have realize that a symbol semicolon(;) is the one you have forgotten to input.
